Market Overview

Face recognition technology is associated with high costs and severe challenges in achieving high accuracy on raw data – Security, surveillance, events etc.

Most of the market players today have trained and tested their algorithm on structured data comprising of European and American faces.

Hence, these algorithms can not deliver high accuracy on raw data especially on dark complexioned faces.

Path Ahead

We at Kwikpic are developing a highly accurate image processing system that is specifically trained to work with raw data and multi-ethnic faces.

Image processing is at a nascent stage with limited application in many countries leaving huge untapped opportunity across industries.

We started by using our Face Recognition system for sorting and delivering event photos.

After validating our technology, now we are all set to enter the retail market in India and then expand to other countries.
